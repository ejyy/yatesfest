Yes, it's actually been a decade since we said "I do"!

To celebrate Ell & Lou's 10th wedding anniversary, we would love you and your family to join us for a weekend of relaxed glamping, organised fun and evenings in the hot tub.

All is on us (we absolutely insist!), including accommodation and food. We'd just love your company to help us celebrate, however please bring whatever you fancy drinking and some nibbles / baking for the dessert table!

Where?

Box Barn, Great Oak Road, Bryngwyn, Raglan NP15 2AF.

Come join us at Box Barn, our exclusive weekend hideaway in Monmouthshire, at the site of an old dairy farm. We have a meadow for camping and games, wood fired pizza oven, BBQ and firepit, a covered barn space / Bothy bar / kitchen and two hot showers. We also have wood-fired hot tub, yoga deck and if so inclined, a cold plunge barrel.

The plan

Friday: Arrive from 5pm, we'll be making homemade pizzas and have an evening by the firepit and wood-fired hot tub.

Saturday: Leisurely breakfast and a morning of games in the meadow. Buffet lunch and then family entertainment before our delivery of fish and chips. The bar will be open with full service to the hot tub. Anyone seen touching the fireside guitar will be evicted.

Sunday: Kiddy-friendly walk across the fields to the local pub for Sunday lunch, then carriages.

Kit list

Campers; please bring all your own camping stuff (tents and sleeping bags etc). If we've organised you a room, then bedding is provided, but please bring a travel cot if you need one. Please bring a torch, wellies, swimming stuff and towels for the hot tub.
